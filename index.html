ISESSAH-SEA 2019 Conference

The International Society for Economics and Social Sciences of Animal Health - South East Asia 2019

17 – 18 October 2019, IPB International Convention Center, IPB University, Indonesia

About The Event

About ISESSAH-SEA 2019

The International Society for Economics and Social Sciences of Animal Health - South East Asia 2019 (ISESSAH-SEA 2019) is a general meeting to connect and update research projects among experts and scientists within the field of social science, animal health and production economics, epidemiologists, veterinarians, medical practitioners and government agencies in the South East Asia region.

Where

IPB International Convention Center, IPB University, Indonesia

When

Thursday to Friday
17 – 18 October 2019

ISESSAH-SEA 2019 Outline

Here is our event outline

Section 1

Roundtable Discussion

Section 2

Paper Presentations

Selected participant in ISESSAH-SEA 2019, will do paper presentation to describe the main subject of their studies.

Section 3

Poster Presentations

Every participant in ISESSAH-SEA 2019, will do poster presentation to describe the main subject of their studies.

The length of poster session/exhibition will be around 1.5-2 hours. For each poster, a standard space of poster size (A0 paper/ 84 x 119 cm) will be provided.

Section 4

Workshop on animal health economics

There will be networking Workshop for all participants on cases as problem based learning
